Electron Internal Builds Wiki

Welcome to the Electron Internal Builds Wiki. This page provides a comprehensive version history of Electron builds created by the Electron team at Microsoft, including currently supported builds and those that are no longer supported. These builds are customized to suit different application needs, environments, and compliance requirements across operating systems. For more information on the builds, the team, or Electron at Microsoft, please visit our team page.
