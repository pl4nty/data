He whakaaetanga raihana tēnei i waenga i a kōrua ko te Microsoft Corporation (tētahi o ana hoa kamupene rānei) e whakamārama ana i ō motika, ō herenga, me ngā āhuatanga e āhei ai koe te whakamahi i te pūmanawa me ngā whakahounga (“Pūmanawa”) (hāunga ki te whānuitanga e tāpirihia ai ki ngā whakahounga ngā ture hou, ngā ture tāpiri rānei, ā, ki te pēnā ka hāngai aua ture ā te wā e heke mai, ā, kāore e whakarerekē i ō motika, i ō Microsoft motika rānei, e pā ana ki ngā Pūmanawa kua whakahoutia tōmuatia ). Me arotake koe i te whakaaetanga katoa, tae atu ki ngā ture e honoa ana, nā te mea he hira ngā ture katoa, ā, huia katoatia ka hanga i tēnei whakaaetanga tiwhikete e āta hāngai ana ki a koe.

Mā te whakaae ki tēnei whakaaetanga, mā te tikiake, mā te whakamahi rānei i te Pūmanawa, e whakaae ana koe ki ēnei ture katoa me te whakaae hoki ki te whakawhitinga o ētahi mōhiohio i roto i tō whakamahi i te pūmanawa e ai ki tō Tauākī Tūmataitinga Windows, ki te Wāhanga 2.7 rānei, i raro. Ki te kore koe e whakaae, e tautuku rānei ki aua ture, tē āhei koe te whakamahi i te Pūmanawa, i ana āhuahira rānei.

E rua ngā wāhanga o tēnei whakaaetanga: E hāngai ana te Wāhanga 1 ina whakamahi koe i te Pūmanawa i tētahi pūhara Windows; E hāngai ana te Wāhanga 2 ki te whakamahia e koe i tētahi pūhara ehara i te Windows, tae atu ki te MacOS, iOS, Android, me Linux.

1.     MICROSOFT EDGE MO NGĀ PŪRERE WINDOWS

1.1.        Ka Hāngai Ngā Ture Raihana Windows

. Kei te whakahaeretia tō whakamahi i tēnei pūmanawa i tētahi pūhara Windows e ō Ture Raihana Pūmanawa Microsoft, e tō Pūnaha Whakahaere Windows (“Ngā Ture Raihana Windows”), ā, he wāhanga ērā o tēnei whakaaetanga. Ka tirohia Ngā Ture Raihana Windows ma te haere ki te https://aka.ms/useterms mā te haere rānei ki Ngā Tautuhinga - Pūnaha - Mō i roto i Windows.

1.1.1.        Ngā whakahou

. Ia wā, ia wā ka tirotiro te pūmanawa mō ngā whakahou, ā, ka tikiake me te tāuta mōu. Me whiwhi whakahoutanga anake koe mai i a Microsoft, i ngā mātāpuna whaimana rānei, ā, mā Microsoft pea e whakahou tō pūnaha hei whakarato i aua whakahoutanga ki a koe. Mā tō whakaae ki tēnei whakaaetanga, mā te whakamahi rānei i te Pūmanawa, e whakaae ana koe kia whiwhi i ēnei whakahou aunoa me te kore e whakamōhio i a koe.

1.2.        Ngā Arokite

. Kei a koe te tikanga ki te whakamahi i ngā putanga arokite, insider, peta, tuku-tōmua, tētahi atu putanga rānei, o te Pūmanawa (“Ngā Arokite”) ka whakawāteatia pea e Microsoft. Ka āhei te whakamahi Arokite tae noa ki te rā e mōnehu ai te Pūmanawa, ā, mēnā e tautuku ana koe ki ngā ture katoa o tēnei whakaaetanga. He putanga whakamātautau noa iho ngā arokite, ka mutu, ka tino rerekē pea i te putanga ka tuku arumonitia. Ahakoa ngā mea tātā pea kei ō Ture Raihana Windows, E whakaratohia ana ngā arokite hei “ĀHUA ONĀIANEI,” me te kore e hāngai o tētahi pūtāhui, ahakoa kua hīkarotia, kua āta kīia ranei (tae atu ki te Pūtāhui Whai Here), ki aua putanga. Mā te tāuta Arokite ki tō pūrere, ka whakakore pea koe i tō pūtāhui pūrere, ka pāngia pea tērā rānei, me tō kore pea e māraurau kia whiwhi tautoko mai i te kaiwhakanao o tō pūrere, i tō kaiwhakahaere whatunga rānei, mēnā kei te hāngai. Kāore a Microsoft e whai haepapa ana ki te tūkino ka pā ki a koe mā te pērā. Kaore pea a Microsoft e tuku ratonga tautoko mō ngā Arokite. Ki te hoatu koe i ngā tākupu, ngā huatau, ētahi atu urupare rānei mō te Arokite ("Tāpaetanga"), ka tuku koe ki a Microsoft me ōna hoa i ngā motika ki te whakamahi i te Tāpaetanga ahakoa te huarahi, te kaupapa rānei o taua whakamahinga.

1.2.1.        Kohinga Raraunga mo ngā Arokite

. Mō ngā Arokite, kāore pea te tūmataitinga me ngā tautuhinga āhuahira e mahi ki tērā ka tūmanakohia, ā, kaore pea ngā Arokite e mahi tahi me ētahi atu tautuhinga tūmataiti Windows, tae atu ki ngā tautuhinga raraunga tātaritanga mō te Windows 10, te Windows 11 rānei. Ka whakamahia, ka tiakina, ka tukatukahia, ka tātarihia ngā raraunga i kohia mai i tō whakamahi i ngā Arokite, tae atu ki ngā raraunga tātari, hangarau, pūrongo hapa, ngā putunga raraunga hinganga, me ētahi atu raraunga whai pānga mai i ō pūrere e whakahaere Arokite ana, kia noho hou tonu, haumaru me te mahi tika a Windows, tēnei Pūmanawa, me ngā Arokite. Ka āwhina hoki i a mātou ki te whakapai ake i ngā hua me ngā ratonga a Microsoft, ā, ka whakamahia hoki mō ētahi atu kaupapa pērā i whakaahuatia i te Tauākī Tūmataiti Microsoft. Mehemea ka monokia e koe te kohikohi raraunga mā ngā mana e wātea ana i ngā Arokite, ka haere tonu pea tā matou kohikohi mōhiohio tataritanga mō te tikiake, mō te tāuta me te tango i ngā Arokite me ngā raraunga "taketake" pēra i whakaahuatia i te wāhanga Tātaritanga Windows o te Tauākī Tūmataiti Microsoft kei https://aka.ms/privacy.

1.3.        Ētahi atu ratong

. Ka whai wāhi pea ki te Pūmanawa ngā taupānga, ngā āhuahira rānei e whakarato ana i te pito urunga, ka whakawhirinaki rānei, ki ngā ratonga tuihono, ā, ka whakahaeretia pea te whakamahinga o aua ratonga e ētahi ture me ngā kaupapahere tūmataiti motuhake, pērā i te Whakaaetanga Ratonga Microsoft kei https://aka.ms/msa. Ka kitea aua ture me ngā kaupapahere mā te tiro i ngā ture whakamahi o te ratonga, ki ngā tautuhinga rānei o te taupānga, mēnā e hāngai ana. Kāore pea e wātea ana ngā ratonga i ngā rohe katoa. Kaua koe e whakamahi i ngā maimai ka whakamahia e te Pūmanawa kia karanga ki tētahi ratonga Microsoft Azure e noho wehe ana i te Pūmanawa.
